A little about Amy

“Writing is the painting of the voice.” -Voltaire

Art is one of my passions in life, which is why I created this blog over three years ago. I write about all genres of art, but am most fascinated by artists using technology, such as virtual reality, robots and artificial intelligence. In addition to blogging, I have contributed articles to several publications, including Professional Artist Magazine, Sculpture Magazine, and Create Hub.

Thank you for visiting my site!
